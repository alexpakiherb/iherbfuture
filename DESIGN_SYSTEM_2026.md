# iHerb Design System — 2026 Brand Refresh

> Source: FigJam board `jQSRtCgkHOM3rZIByWrQIc` — iHerb Specialty Store Key Pages

## Brand Colors

### Primary — "Evergreen"
- **Brand Primary:** `#0A6B3C` (Header, logo, CTA, badges) — replaces old `#458500`
- **Primary Dark:** `#065C32` (Hover states)
- **Primary Light:** `#E5F8E6` (Active states, pills)

### Secondary
- **Action Orange:** `#D14800` (Promotions, discounts, sale, "Add" button)
- **Trust Blue:** `#1558A6` (Links, trust badges, credibility)

### Neutrals
- White: `#FFFFFF`, Gray BG: `#F7F8F7`
- Text Primary: `#333333`, Text Secondary: `#666666`
- Border: `#CCCCCC`, Divider: `#E0E0E0`
- Dark (footer): `#212529`
- Star Rating: `#F5A623`

### Category Colors
Supplements (`#79A83C`), Sports (`#1E65D6`), Bath (`#148777`), Beauty (`#6E46C8`), Grocery (`#B38900`), Home (`#454B49`), Baby (`#3D93BF`), Pets (`#083833`)

## Typography
- Font: Noto Sans (400, 700) — only one font family
- H1: 26px Bold, H2: 22px Bold, H3: 20px Bold, H4: 18px Bold

## Key Components
- Pill-shaped badges, buttons, search bar (border-radius: 9999px)
- Product cards: vertical (grid) and horizontal (list) views
- 3-tier header: promo bar + nav bar + category subnav
- Filter sidebar: 240px, collapsible sections
- Dark footer with green accent stripe

## Strategic Pillars
1. Trust Amplifiers — 3rd party testing, quality promise
2. Curated Merchandising — expert picks, bundles
3. Guided Customer Experience — health goals, AI overviews
4. Personalization — smart synergies, daily reveals
5. Better Look and Feel — this brand refresh
